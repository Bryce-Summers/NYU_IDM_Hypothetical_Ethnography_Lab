Brooklyn Ethnographic Research Center

The academic programs in Integrated Digital Media Ethnogrophy Lab (IDMEL) at the Hockenberry School of Ethnography consist of both Bachelors and Masters of Arts degrees, as well as an accelerated BA/MA program. In these programs, digital media faculty and students from the NYU integrated Digital Media program are experimentally observed and explored in their spectrum of practices ranging from computer programming to 2D and 3D graphics to photography, film, and audio. We provide the opportunity for students to conduct research on the lives of artists, designers, and creative technologists who are working in highly fluid industries that reward creativity, throughfullness, technical expertise, and innovation. Through our observation of the IDM students, our students have access to the widest range of observable subjects with varying interests out of any introspective ethnogrophy lab in the country.
